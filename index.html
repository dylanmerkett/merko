How it works

Hello, I'm Merko, your new web services professional. I know you’re busy, helping others with their health and fitness goals, so let me take care of your website and online presence. I'm really good at it, I promise!

Discover

I want to know all about you! What your dreams and ambitions are for your business, why you're unique, what your message is and who you want it to reach. I'll even come and take part in a class!

Design

Using the information from the discovery, I'll now create a design of your homepage for your review and input. This is a partnership so I'll want to hear your thoughts throughout the design process.

Develop

When you're happy with the design, I'll start to turn it into a working website. Throughout development, you'll have a preview URL where you can begin to test your site across all devices.

Content

Your customers will visit your website for a reason so let's make sure we can answer their questions. I'll lay your content out so it looks beautiful and is optimised for the search engines.

Launch

Time to celebrate! But our work's not done yet. We now need to promote your site so the right people find it. I'll also make sure you're fully trained so you can make updates to your site.
