Free consultation Why Merko

From blank canvass to a website that works

I know you’re busy, helping people achieve their goals, so let me take care of your website. Here's my process for a simple website design and build.

Discover

I want to know all about you and your business. Your dreams and ambitions, why you're unique and who you want to reach. I'll even come to one of your classes!

Design

Using this information, I'll create a design of your homepage for your review and input. This is a partnership so I'll want to hear your thoughts throughout the design process.

Develop

When you're happy with the design, I'll start to turn it into a working website. You'll be able to preview and test your site and get a feel for the finished product.

Content

You can send me your content or we can create it together. Either way, I'll lay your content out so it looks great, is easy to read and optimised for the search engines.

Launch

Once your new website is checked and tested, and Google knows where it is, I'll make your site live to the world and set it to work, helping you grow your business and build your brand.

Promote

Let's not stop now though! I'll make sure your shiny new website reaches the people who are looking for someone with your skills and expertise.

Why Merko

My name is Dylan (Merko to my friends) and I specialise in helping health and fitness business owners reach more people by creating beautiful websites that work. I’m a published Sport Science BSc with a history in health physiology but for the past 10 years, I’ve created content and managed websites in the healthcare industry, so I have the perfect mix of skills and experience to help you.
It’s not enough to create a great looking website and hope that people visit it. I cover a full range of services, from helping you understand the goals of your website, to promoting it to the people who are out there looking for you. As a small business owner, you know the importance of a website but it’s often hard to find the time to take it to the next level. This is were I come in.
As well as designing and building your site, I can manage your content and social media channels or just be there to support you. Whatever your situation, we can come up with a solution.
I’m as passionate about people living fit and healthy lifestyles as you are so I want to share my web skills and experience to help you create a successful online presence.
