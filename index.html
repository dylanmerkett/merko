Here's the process for a typical website design and build.

Discover

I want to know all about you! What your dreams and ambitions are, why you're unique, what your message is and who you want it to reach. I'll even come and take part in one of your classes!

Design

Using the information we've gathered, I'll now create a design of your homepage for your review and input. This is a partnership so I'll want to hear your thoughts throughout the design process.

Develop

When you're happy with the design, I'll start to turn it into a working website. Throughout development, you'll have a preview URL where you can begin to test your site & get a feel for the finished product.

Content

People will come to your website for a reason, so let's make sure we can answer their questions. I'll lay your content out so it looks great, is easy to read and optimised for the search engines.

Launch

We've checked the content, tested the site and made sure Google knows where it is. Now it's time to make your site live to the world and set it to work, helping you grow your business and build your brand.

Promote

Now the fun can really begin! It's time to start creating content that speaks directly to your customers and start marketing your new site on all of the right mediums, so you reach the people looking for your services.
