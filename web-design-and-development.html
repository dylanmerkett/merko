Web design and development

Having a website and having a website that helps your fitness business grow are two different things. It's time to stop thinking about your website as a 'virtual business card' and start thinking of it as an effective online marketing channel.

We create websites for fitness professionals that not only look beautiful but also achieve your business goals as well as your website's visitor's goals.

This is achieved through a 'strategy first' process, which means we take the time to get to know you and your business. We take time to understand your perfect customer and the goals and dreams you have for your business. We then take this information and turn it into a design that will build trust and encourage people to engage with your business.

Throughout the whole process, you can be involved as much or as little as you want. Being a small business as well, I aim to flexible to your needs.

Once your website is live, you'll receive training so that you can keep it regularly. I can also put together a content package for you so you can concentrate on your customers safe in the knowledgw that your website ios being cared for by an expert.

Because we only build websites for fitness professionals, it means you get an online service that understands your industry and has and extensive and detailed knowledge of your situation.

Anyone can create websites, at Merko, we create effective websites that are focused on results.
